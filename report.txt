2. АРХИТЕКТУРА CNN
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Структура:
  - Входной слой: 62x47x1 (grayscale)
  - Блок 1: Conv2D(32) → BatchNorm → Conv2D(32) → MaxPool → Dropout(0.25)
  - Блок 2: Conv2D(64) → BatchNorm → Conv2D(64) → MaxPool → Dropout(0.25)

  - Flatten
  - Dense(256) → BatchNorm → Dropout(0.5)
  - Dense(128) → BatchNorm → Dropout(0.5)
